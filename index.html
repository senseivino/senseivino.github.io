Vino's Wisdoms

Vino’s Principles

  1. Wisdom is not the sum of what you know works, but rather the sum of what you know does not.
  2. The problem with mediocre minds is that they're clever enough to know that they're right, but not clever enough to know that they're not.

Vino’s Rules

  1. Just because you learned one thing, does not mean it applies to everything.
  2. Just because you can, does not mean you should.
  3. You will always be wrong. If not today, then in the future.
  4. What works there, often does not work here.
  5. Doing what you 'know' locks you into the past.
